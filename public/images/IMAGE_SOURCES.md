## Testimonial avatars

No stock avatar illustration could be reliably sourced under a confirmed free-commercial license during this pass
(Pixabay blocked automated access). To avoid using real people's photos without consent, testimonial avatars are
rendered as initials badges in the brand palette (see `components/Testimonials.tsx`) instead of image files.

## Gallery before/after treatment

"Before" images are intentionally rendered in grayscale (CSS filter) and "after" images in full color — a deliberate
design choice that also smooths over the fact that these are generic stock placeholders rather than matched
photo pairs of the same room.
